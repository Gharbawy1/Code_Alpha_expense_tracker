Frequently Asked Questions

An expense tracker is a tool designed to help individuals or businesses monitor and manage their financial expenditures. It allows users to record and categorize their expenses, providing insights into spending patterns and helping to maintain a balanced budget.

To use the expense tracker, simply sign up for an account on our website, log in, and start recording your expenses. Input details such as the amount spent, date, and category. You can also customize expense categories to match your specific needs. The system will then organize and display your financial data for easy analysis.

The expenses format in the dashboard provides a clear overview of your spending. It typically includes a breakdown of expenses by categories, a timeline of transactions, and graphical representations of your financial data. This format helps you visualize where your money is going, making it easier to identify areas for potential savings or budget adjustments.
